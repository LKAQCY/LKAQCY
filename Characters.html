Characters

As the game progresses.

We will be faced with the choice of following one of the three lords. This is a great opportunity to decide which direction you want to play.

The three options here correspond to the three talent trees that focus on combat, life, and social.

You will be given similar choices from time to time, and they follow a trend: the commitments you make will determine the optional goals, and those goals will accelerate your progress as you complete them.
